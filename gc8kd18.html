Geocache Description:


Geocache is NOT located at the posted coordinates, but it is hidden within Spitler Woods with permission.

Putting this cache together was a lot of fun. Although, it did take quite a bit of work to find a suitable hiding place. Looking for this cache should also be fun since it's a nice hike in one of the best parks in the area. Included below are coordinates for good parking. Not too far along a well established trail, you will find this cache tucked away nicely in its hiding spot. Don't try to find this cache after 10pm since the park is closed at that time. Relax and take your time to explore the park and hike around, it's a great place! On the off chance that you have kids with you, the playground is a great place to stop. My kids love coming here to play. Eventually, after you've found the cache and signed the logbook, be sure to place it back as you found it so others will enjoy it all the same.
